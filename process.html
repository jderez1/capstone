How did I go about tackling this problem? The first step was of course creating a brand. And a brand also comes with guidelines. These brand guidelines have been the first step towards the bigger picture.

The logo

The logo is a modern, stylized text "pickpal" with a graphic resembling a mouse click, combining cool blue and vibrant green colors, symbolizing connectivity, technology, and innovation. The logo mark is a graphic resembling a mouse click, combining cool blue and vibrant green colors, symbolizing connectivity, technology, and innovation.

Brand typography

I chose Futura for Pickpal for its sleek, modern aesthetics and geometric clarity, reflecting the brand's commitment to innovation and its forward-thinking approach in a single, instantly recognizable visual statement. I chose Roboto to pair with Futura because its humanist qualities and open curves offer a warm, readable contrast to Futura's stark geometry, ensuring legibility and a contemporary feel in my brand's visual communication.
