MAKING CHOICES EASY

"I developed a subscription based product recommendation quiz software that companies can integrate into their webshop to make it easy for their customers to find the right products and offers personalized guidance and support."

Is there a problem?

The challenge of choice

In todays digital age, e-commerce has become a cornerstone of retail, offering consumers unparalleled convenience and access to a vast array of products. However, with this abundance of choice comes the challenge of decision fatigue and information overload for online shoppers.

Customer frustration

Navigating through extensive product catalogs to find the most suitable items can be overwhelming and time-consuming, leading to frustration and decreased satisfaction among consumers.

Business Dilemma

Moreover, e-commerce businesses face their own set of challenges in providing personalized assistance to customers in real-time, resulting in missed opportunities for upselling and cross-selling.

Inefficient Processes

Traditional search and filter functionalities often fall short in delivering tailored recommendations that truly resonate with individual preferences and needs. At its core, the problem lies in the inefficiency of current decision-making processes in e-commerce.

The solution

The software is a selection aid/ product recommendation quiz to make it easy for their customers to find the right products.
