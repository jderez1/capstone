Understanding the need for Pickpal

My journey to understanding the necessity of PickPal began with personal experiences in navigating the complexities of e-commerce. While working on my Cook & Sizzle project, which involved managing a cooking webshop with over 3,000 products, I encountered the pervasive issue of product overload. This realization prompted a deeper exploration into the broader e-commerce landscape, where I discovered a common challenge: the difficulty for consumers in finding the right products amidst overwhelming choices.
